[b]Basic Automated Mining (BAM) is a simple, lightweight veinminer alternative.[/b]

[b]Comes with:[/b]
• No hotkeys, just mine ores to vein mine
• 50% smaller packet size
• No complex tile scanning
• Dynamic tile breaking limits
• Very lightweight

[b]Additional features:[/b]
• Fully multiplayer compatible
• Right-click toggle auto mining
• Simple whitelist w/ Modded tile support
• Middle-click (scroll wheel) + Right-click easy whitelisting
• Localized translation settings & messages
• Extra config settings to play with
